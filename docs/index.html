Hi, I’m Nhan.

I am a PhD candidate in Accounting at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M), currently on the job market for 2024-2025. I was a visiting scholar at UNC-Chapel Hill from January to May 2024.

My research interest focuses on contemporary reporting and auditing issues related to technology, society, and sustainability. To support that, I specialize intensively in data science methods such as machine learning, deep learning, and generative AI, which I apply to both corporate reporting and alternative data sources.

I’m ready to teach financial accounting and auditing courses, leveraging my professional and teaching background. I’m also keen to lead research and data-oriented courses, supervise student theses, and mentor PhD students as needed.
